Guidelines for Submissions

Our goal is to make Wikivinci a very high quality database of learning materials. That'll only happen with your help. Take a minute and review the following guidelines before submitting.

  • Avoid submitting news. Your submission should be useful for a long period of time.
  • Avoid submitting products. Submitting books, courses, etc. that need to be purchased is encouraged, but submitting "Adobe Photoshop" is not.
  • Any format (videos, slides, blog post, etc.) is fine, just make sure the learning value is explicit within the material.
